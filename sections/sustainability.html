Sustainability

All of our products are made with at least 80% recycled materials

We are committed to reducing the environmental impact of the fashion industry.

Did you know that during 2020, 164 million online purchase orders were made in Argentina? If we think about the packaging that this implies, we find ourselves with a huge problem of waste of containers and wrappers. As part of our environmental commitment, we choose to transport the products from one point to another wrapped in 100% compostable packaging made with mainly organic raw materials: paper and cardboard. The boxes for sale online are made up of 70% recycled material and the bags for sale at the store are made of FSC-certified paper. In addition to being biodegradable, they are easy to recycle. When designing, we use the least amount of ink possible so that the packaging can be composted efficiently.
